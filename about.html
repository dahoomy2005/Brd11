Who are we (me)

We are Barad, a UAE-based organization operating in accordance with Islamic principles. We provide services such as website and app design, Graphic Design, motion graphics, and animation.

How we do things...

Honesty


At Barad,
we value honesty and integrity above all else.
We do not tolerate lying, as we are guided by the teaching of
Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ):
"Beware of lying, for lying leads to immorality, and immorality leads to Hell." (Sahih Bukhari and Muslim)
We believe that truthfulness is the foundation of trust, and trust is the key to building meaningful and successful relationships.

Our Commitment to Timeliness


At Barad, we value efficiency and respect for time.
We believe that unnecessary delays can undermine trust and professionalism, which is why we prioritize fulfilling our commitments promptly and responsibly.

Trust and Privacy


At Bard, we hold trust and privacy as core principles. We ensure the utmost care in safeguarding sensitive information and fulfilling our commitments.We are guided by the words of

Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ):
"The signs of a hypocrite are three: when he speaks, he lies; when he promises, he breaks it; and when he is entrusted, he betrays." (Sahih Bukhari and Muslim)
